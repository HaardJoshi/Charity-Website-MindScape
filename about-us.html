Our Story



Frustrated by the lack of support for children facing mental health challenges, a group of dedicated individuals – John Jacob, Jan Johnson and Tuva Marshall– came together, united by a common purpose. They saw a generation struggling in silence, their potential dimmed by anxiety, depression, and a lack of resources.

Believing there had to be a better way, they joined forces in 2023. Their vision? To create a world where every child could access the tools and support they needed to thrive. Fueled by their shared passion, they built MindScape, a groundbreaking organization dedicated to revolutionizing children's mental health support. We brought innovative strategies, evidence-based practices, and unwavering compassion to the fight. Since our founding, we've made significant strides.

We've impacted over 20,000 young lives by providing access to mental health services, training professionals, and much more. Our commitment to early intervention, accessible care, and breaking the stigma surrounding mental health is changing the landscape for children everywhere.

With your help, we can ensure millions of children around the world , have the opportunity to flourish, free from the invisible chains of mental health challenges.
