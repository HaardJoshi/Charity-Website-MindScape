Exam Stress & Eating Disorder: A Journey to Balance



avg_pace

4 min read



Author:

Sarah (20)



The Challenge:

High school exams loomed, and Sarah grappled with the pressure along with a pre-existing eating disorder. The stress fueled unhealthy habits, creating a vicious cycle.



The Turning Point:

Sarah discovered a supportive online community where other young people shared experiences with anxiety and eating disorders. Through this community, she found tips for managing stress, like mindfulness exercises and relaxation techniques. She also connected with a therapist who helped her develop a healthy relationship with food and cope with exam pressure.



The Success:

Sarah learned to prioritize self-care amidst the exam frenzy. She balanced studying with relaxation techniques and healthy eating habits. With the support of her online community and therapist, she faced the exams confidently and achieved good results, all while nurturing her physical and mental well-being.



Key Takeaways:

You're not alone! Support communities and professional help can make a huge difference. Prioritize self-care during stressful times, and remember, good grades don't define your worth.

" For a very long time, I couldn’t say the word ‘trauma’. I couldn’t even write or type it. I felt guilty for the word even being associated with my experiences. "
